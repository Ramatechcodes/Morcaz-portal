About Morcaz Uloom

Morcaz Uloom Arabiyyat Islamiyyat Ejigbo Lagos was established in 1986 as a center of excellence for Islamic and Arabic studies.

The institution has produced thousands of graduates who have gone on to become respected scholars, teachers, and professionals.

The school combines traditional Islamic scholarship with modern educational practices to prepare students for leadership and service in society.

Founder

Sheikh Muizdeen Salmon Alawiye

Sheikh Muizdeen Salmon Alawiye popularly known as Baba Legigbo is the founder of Morcaz Uloom Arabiyyat Islamiyyat Ejigbo Lagos.

For more than four decades he has dedicated his life to the teaching of the Qur'an, Arabic language and Islamic scholarship.

Thousands of students have graduated under his mentorship and are now scholars, teachers and community leaders in Nigeria and across the world.

Our Vision

Our vision is to build a world class Islamic and Arabic educational institution that nurtures knowledgeable, disciplined and morally upright individuals who will contribute

positively to society.


We aim to combine traditional Islamic scholarship with modern academic excellence so that our students can compete globally while maintaining strong Islamic values.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high quality Islamic and Arabic education that empowers students with knowledge, character and leadership.

We strive to develop scholars who are grounded in the Qur'an and Sunnah while also equipping them with modern skills needed to succeed in today's world.
